## Getting started

The badge runs [MicroPythonOS](https://docs.micropythonos.com/) by default and comes with a number of [pre-installed applications](https://docs.micropythonos.com/apps/built-in-apps/) and an [app store](https://docs.micropythonos.com/apps/appstore/) where you can find many additional programs.


## Reset Default Firmware

Is your badge no longer booting, or do you want to go back to the original software? Head over to the badge / soldering tent where you will find the badge repair station. On this PC you can follow step-by-step instructions to re-flash all boards. Or see [here how to reset it yourself](https://install.micropythonos.com/)
